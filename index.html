What is GearTrack?

GearTrack is a curated sports equipment hub that brings together essential gear across popular athletic categories. From basketballs and cleats to rackets and jerseys, this site highlights a variety of items used in everyday sports environments. Designed with clarity and structure in mind, GearTrack offers a visual introduction to the equipment that powers the games we play.
